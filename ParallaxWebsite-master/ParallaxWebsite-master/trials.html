9 Circles of Hell

Descend into Dante's Inferno

1.Limbo

Eternal Rest for Virtuous Unbaptized Souls

The first circle of Hell is reserved for virtuous pagans and unbaptized infants. They are neither punished nor tormented but are deprived of the joy of being in the presence of God.

2.Lust

The second circle is where the sin of lust is punished

Here, the souls of the lustful are trapped in a tempestuous storm, constantly tossed about by strong winds.

3.Gluttony

The realm of gluttony

The third circle is the realm of gluttony. Those who overindulged in food and drink during their earthly lives are punished by being forced to lie in a vile slush, enduring a never-ending rain of filth and garbage

4.Greed

In the fourth circle, the souls of the greedy and the wasteful are punished.

They are divided into two groups: those who hoarded wealth and those who squandered it. The greedy souls push heavy weights against each other, while the wasteful souls must watch their possessions roll back and forth.

5.Wrath

The fifth circle is where the wrathful and the sullen are punished

The wrathful are submerged in the River Styx, endlessly attacking and biting each other. The sullen are trapped beneath the water, forever in a state of sadness and gloom.

6.Heresy

The sixth circle is the realm of heretics

Here, the souls of those who held beliefs contrary to the teachings of the Church are trapped in fiery tombs, burning eternally.

7.Violence

The seventh circle is divided into three rings, each punishing a different form of violence.

The violent against others are submerged in a river of boiling blood. The violent against themselves are transformed into trees and fed upon by harpies. The violent against God, nature, or art are subjected to a burning desert and falling fire.

8.Fraud

The eighth circle is the realm of fraud

It is further divided into ten concentric ditches, each punishing a specific form of fraud. The punishments include immersion in a lake of boiling tar, being whipped by demons, or walking endlessly while wearing heavy burdens.

9.Treachery

The final and ninth circle is reserved for the most severe sin of treachery.

It is divided into four rounds, each punishing a different form of betrayal. Traitors to their kin, country, guests, and benefactors are frozen in a lake of ice, with their bodies contorted in painful positions
